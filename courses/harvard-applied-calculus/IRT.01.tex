	Question 01:
	Compute\\
	\begin{align*}
		\lim_{\theta \to \infty} P(\theta)
	\end{align*}
	\\
	Answer:
	\begin{align*}
		\lim_{\theta \to \infty} P(\theta)\\
		&=\lim_{\theta \to \infty} {c + \frac{1-c}{1+e^{-a(\theta - b)}}}\\
		&=\lim_{\theta \to \infty} {c + \frac{1-c}{1}}\\
		&=\lim_{\theta \to \infty} {c + 1 -c}\\
		&=\lim_{\theta \to \infty} {1}\\
		&=1
	\end{align*}
