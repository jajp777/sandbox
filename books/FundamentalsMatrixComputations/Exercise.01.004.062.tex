	Exercise 1.4.62\\
	Prove that if A is positive definite, then $det(A) > 0$.\\
	\\
	Proof:\\
	\\
	\begin{align*}
		A &= R^TT\\
		det(A) &= det(R^TR)\\
		det(A) &= det(R^T)det(R)\\		
		det(A) &= det(R^T)\prod{a_{ii}}\\		
		det(A) &= \prod{a_{ii}}\prod{a_{ii}}\\				
		det(A) &= (\prod{a_{ii}})^2\\			
		det(A) &> 0\\					
		\square
	\end{align*}
